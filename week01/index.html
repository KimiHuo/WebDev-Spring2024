Guide For How to Make Salad

--Kimi Huo

This is a brief note about how to make a vegetable salad.

What we need:

  • some vegetables
  • salad dressings

    • Step1: Wash Your Greens

    Washing your ingredients is always one of the most important aspects. After all, no one wants to use dirt as a seasoning or find some "meat" in their dish.

    Step2: Cut Your Vegetables

    Cut them into smaller pieces if they are big to get into yoour mouth.

    Step3: Mix Them

    Combine the veggies with the dressing, and any other dressing you want to use (usually pepper, bits of vanilla, sugar, etc. If you want have some spicy in your salad, that's fine, I will not stop you from that) into a bowl.

    Now You Can Enjoy Your Salad!

    Try to put your salad into your mouth.
